Short Narrative About Yourself

Zynarah O. Mangui

August 27,2021

My name is Zynarah O. Mangui, my parents and relatives call me Arah for short. I am 16 years old, born on July 27, 2005 in Boac, Marinduque. I completed my Junior High School in Marinduque Academy Inc. The Mangui clans in Mogpog is known for their talents in singing and playing instruments, mine is for dancing. I was a member of my Junior High School Dance Group called Saliw Maraca Dance Troupe and I actively participated in every event and competitions. One of the competitions our dance group participated in twice was the Dancesports Competition held in Boac, Marinduque on December 2018 and 2019. I was in the Latin Category. Sad to say, we didn’t win first place both times instead we got second place. I was happy but not satisfied. Other than that, I also participated in the Street Dancing Competition as one of the members of the Mogpog Team held in Boac, Marinduque during the Marinduque Centennial Celebration were we emerged as champion. To state the obvious, I love and enjoy dancing and I am passionate doing it.
In my free time, I like to play online games like Genshin Impact, and sometimes paint. But my favorite thing to do in my free time is to spend time with my cute, fluffy and chubby dog. My family and I are dog lovers. We have 6 dogs. One of them is named Haru and I give special attention to her. Another thing I like to do in my free time is watch movies together with my family.
I plan to pursue the course Accountancy in college as I want to be a CPA (Certified Public Accountant) so that I can have a financially stable and happy life as I grow old. I am a simple and optimistic person. For now, I am contented with what I have and I always look at the bright side of life and focus on the positive things.
