Writing a Concept Paper

QUESTION 1: WHAT CONCEPT IS BEING EXPLAINED?

The concept is about fostering mental health through social relationships interaction. It explains and shows the connection between social relationships and the different aspects of health and wellness.

QUESTION 2: WHAT EXAMPLES ARE GIVEN TO EXPLAIN THE CONCEPT OF THE VIDEO?

There were a number of examples given in the concept video about fostering mental health through social relationships, however, I will only mention three. The following are the examples:
a)Connection of social relationships and the different aspects of health and wellness.
b) Depression and loneliness are associated with inadequate social support
c) The role of sociologists in establishing the connection between social relationships and health outcomes

QUESTION 3: DO THESE EXAMPLES REFLECT REALITIES IN LIFE? EXPLAIN.

Yes, the examples reflect realities in life even before the pandemic. Sadly, studies show or reports that increasing mental health problems happens during the pandemic because of the health protocols or restrictions.

QUESTION 4: IF YOU WERE TO BE MADE A CONCEPT PAPER LIKE THIS, WHAT DO YOU THINK IS THE BEST TITLE FOR THIS?

The original title of the concept video is appropriate but if I were to make another title for it or if I were to be made a concept paper like this, it will be The Relevance of Social Relationships in Nurturing Mental Well-being.
